About Star Electric

Regina's Electrical Professionals

Star Electric is a Regina-based electrical contracting company serving property managers, developers, and businesses across Saskatchewan. We specialize in safe, quality electrical work — from tenant improvements to full commercial installations.

Our team of licensed journeyman electricians takes pride in every project. We communicate clearly, show up on time, and deliver work that lasts. Whether it's a new outlet, a panel upgrade, or a full buildout, Star Electric is the team to call.

Based in Regina, we understand the unique needs and requirements of Saskatchewan commercial properties — and we're committed to helping our community power their businesses safely.
